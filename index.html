how long does it take for delivery?

It takes 4 minutes (order to oven) + 6 minutes in the oven + 5 minutes (cutting, packing, pickup) + 8 minutes (delivery) = 23 minutes. Rest 7 minutes we keep as a buffer for our executives owing to unforeseen circumstances. Hence, the 30 minutes pizza home delivery promise.
